Professional Strongman

Celebrating the World's Strongest Athletes

Oleksii Novikov

Ukraine

Oleksii Novikov became the youngest World's Strongest Man champion in 2020 at just 24 years old. Known for his explosive speed and power, he dominated events like the Atlas Stones and Car Walk.

  • World's Strongest Man 2020
  • World's Ultimate Strongman Champion
  • Europe's Strongest Man podium finisher

Tom Stoltman

Scotland

Tom Stoltman has cemented himself as one of the greatest strongmen of all time with three World's Strongest Man titles. Standing at 6'8", he is a dominant force in Atlas Stones and loading events.

  • 3x World's Strongest Man (2021, 2022, 2024)
  • Europe's Strongest Man winner
  • World record holder in Atlas Stones

Brian Shaw

United States

Brian Shaw is a four-time World's Strongest Man and one of the most accomplished strongmen in history. At 6'8" and over 400 lbs, he is renowned for his incredible deadlift strength and dominance in heavy loading events.

  • 4x World's Strongest Man (2011, 2013, 2015, 2016)
  • Arnold Strongman Classic champion
  • Renowned for Hummer tire deadlift performances
